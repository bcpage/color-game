# Room 00078 — The Hallway

## What it is
An infinite institutional corridor. You are always walking toward the light at the end. You never arrive.

## Mechanic
Canvas animation draws concentric perspective frames scrolling toward the viewer. A vanishing point glows faintly at the center. A distance counter increments but the destination never changes. Pure atmosphere — no interaction.
